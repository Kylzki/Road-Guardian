About Us

Road Guardian is a dedicated platform committed to enhancing cyclist safety. Through our innovative website, we aim to create a safer environment for cyclists by facilitating accident reporting and fostering cyclist community interaction. Our vision is to make cycling a confident and secure experience for riders everywhere. With our crowdsourced accident reporting feature, we enable cyclists to report incidents swiftly, ensuring prompt responses from authorities.

Additionally, we connect cyclists with local events, group rides, and like-minded enthusiasts, promoting a sense of community within the cycling world. Join us at Road Guardian to empower cyclist safety and make cycling an even more enjoyable experience, knowing that we have your back.
